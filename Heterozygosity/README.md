## Genome-wide Heterozygosity
Scripts for computing and visualizing genome-wide heterozygosity 
across populations and timepoints.
- Genomewide_het_boxplots.R: Boxplots of genome-wide heterozygosity by population group and timepoint
- Het_sum_stat.R: Summary statistics for expected heterozygosity across all population groups
- Coverage_summaries.R: Sequencing coverage summaries for standard and deep sequencing datasets

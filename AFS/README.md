## Allele Frequency Spectrum Analysis
Scripts for analyzing allele frequencies at pseudo-fixed sites using 
deep sequencing data and computing founder frequency distributions.
- AFS_deep_seq_GLM.R: GLM analysis of deep sequencing allele frequencies at pseudo-fixed sites
- FounderFreq_Analysis.R: Summary statistics for founder allele frequencies at significant SNPs
- Rare_alleles.R: Simulation of rare allele detectability under standard and deep sequencing coverage
- Fixation.R: Assessment of fixation patterns across trajectory populations

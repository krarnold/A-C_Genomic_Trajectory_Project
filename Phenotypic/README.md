## Phenotypic Analyses
Scripts for analysis and visualization of life history phenotypes 
including larval development timing and age-specific mortality.
- Larval Development 2024.R: Analysis of pupariation timing data from 2024 collections
- Larval Development 2019.R: Analysis of pupariation timing data from 2019 collections
- Pupa2019Stats.R: Statistical tests for pupariation timing, 2019
- Pupa2024Stats.R: Statistical tests for pupariation timing, 2024
- CombinedMortalityAllGraphs.R: Combined mortality trajectory plots across all three timepoints
- Mortality Stats.R: Statistical analysis of age-specific mortality rate differences
- Load Three Mortality Datasets.R: Loads and merges mortality data from 2019, 2020, and 2023 collections
- Wave of Convergence and Graphs.R: Wave-of-convergence analysis and associated figures
